About Us

  • Established in 2004 with strong industry experience.
  • Specialize in manufacturing and installing rolling shutters for shops, homes, and industries.
  • Use premium GI and polycarbonate materials for long-lasting performance.
  • Offer a wide range of shutter types: Push Pull, Gear, Motorized, Round, Full Flat, Half Flat, and Perforated.
  • Provide smooth operation, durability, and low maintenance in every design.
  • Successfully installed more than 1,00,000 shutters across South India.
  • Deliver services to Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Pondicherry, and Andhra Pradesh.
  • Focus on high quality, fast delivery, and customer satisfaction.
  • Build long-term customer relationships through reliable service and fair pricing.
